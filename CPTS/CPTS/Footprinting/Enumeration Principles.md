
OSINT is based exclusively on passive information gathering. 

Only after identifying targets in the client's infrastructure, we need to examine the individual services and protocols. 

Need to understand how the company is structured, what services, and third party vendors it uses, what security measures may be in place, and more. 

The goal is not to get at the systems but to find all the ways to get there. 

- What can we see?
- What reasons can we have for seeing it?
- What image does what we see create for us?
- What do we gain from it?
- How can we use it?
- What can we not see?
- What reasons can there be that we do not see?
- What image results for us from what we do not see?

The core task is not to exploit the machines but to find how they can be exploited. 

1. There is more than meets the eye. Consider all points of view. 
2. Distinguish between what we see and what we do not see. 
3. There are always ways to gain more information. Understand the target.
